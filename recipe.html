Cheesy Grits and Eggs

by Sydne Barard

I was a little girl when my grandfather first made this dish for me and I've been hooked ever since. This is my favorite breakfast to make and it is super simple, quick, and easy.

Ingredients for 1 serving

  • 1 egg
  • Instant Grits
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 slice of Cheddar or American Cheese
  • Butter
  • Salt

What to do

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a pot and add salt
  2. Once water is boiling, add 1/4 cup of instant grits
  3. Lower heat and cover, stirring occasionally
  4. Preheat saucer on stove to medium heat
  5. Add olive oil to pan
  6. Crack and beat egg in bowl, add seasoning
  7. Add egg to saucer and scramble
  8. Check on grits, remove from heat when done and grains are tender
  9. Add butter to the grits
  10. Mix eggs and grits in a bowl
  11. Add slice of cheese and mix until melted
  12. All done! Enjoy!
